Stage Data
Copy all of the text below and paste it into the "Stage Store" field in your settings panel.

  1. Copy the content of the Stage field here - it's all the data you modified. You can copy it with the or by marking it inside the input.
  2. Go to your Settings Panel on the right of the editor. Open the "Onboarding" category if it is not open already.
  3. At the bottom you will find a textarea with a label of "Stages Store". Click inside of the area and paste everything.
  4. If there is something inside make sure you remove it first.
  5. Thats it!!
